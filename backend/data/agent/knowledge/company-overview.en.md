# Aminyx — AI-powered agentic business infrastructure

## Product introduction

**Aminyx** is Hooshyar Daneh Raya Faraz's flagship initiative for **Agentic Infrastructure** and **AI-powered business departments**. It began jointly with Bosch Germany's co-founder / CTO cohort to create full-stack automation for commerce, international trade, and complex B2B interactions.

Funding support later arrived from **Google Startups** and **Amazon**. After iterating on organizational needs and go-to-market fit, product direction pivoted toward **Agentic Organizational Departments**.

## Vision

Aminyx aims at a generation of departmental stacks where sizable portions of operational, analytical, and communication routines are delegated to autonomous agents and workflows. AI is intentionally **not framed as-only a chat UI** — it behaves as an **operational intelligence layer**.

The roadmap explicitly avoids “replace humans entirely”; instead objectives include reducing operational friction, increasing productivity through targeted automation of defined workflows, and guiding leadership decisions via AI assistance.

### Core department layout

Agentic departmental modules include:

1. AI Marketing Department  
2. AI Sales Department  
3. AI CRM Department  
4. AI Data & BI Department  
5. AI Workflow & Automation Layer

## AI Sales Department

Sales agents automate analysis and negotiation choreography. Typical capabilities cited:

- Profiling prospective customers  
- Harmonizing SKU / pricing spreadsheets with CRM knowledge  
- Guiding scripted negotiations anchored to institutional knowledge bases  
- Issuing provisional quotes progressing toward invoicing checkpoints  

Negotiation assistants blend organization-specific rules, CRM dossiers, product catalogs, and pricing ladders. Governance checkpoints (management approval, live pricing snapshots, credentialized paperwork) finalize deals.

# Raima — organizational automation fabric

## Introduction

**Raima** unifies workflows and agents to simplify internal bureaucracy: marketing ingestion, CRM hygiene, BI reporting, payroll / commission calculators, alerts, approvals, lightweight HR ticketing, supplier logistics, costing models, hospitality operations, procurement, franchise rollups — all converging into conversational operators.

## Capability highlights

Marketing qualified leads ingestion, round-robin sales assignment, KPI dashboards, managerial alerts, realtime exports, conversational approvals for finance + HR ticketing.

## Intelligent AI overlays

Operational copilots auto-generate management digests, raise bottlenecks, surface anomaly alerts.

## Accessible interaction surfaces

Primary operator channels emphasize Telegram bots, conversational web dashboards, streamlined admin tiles.

## Reference deployment

Industrial automotive retail group (**Salamatian**) — eight branches spanning Isfahan + Tehran leveraging 50+ staff on daily operational footing. Hospitality / supply-chain / costing variants also piloted.
